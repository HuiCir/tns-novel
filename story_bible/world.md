# World Setting — 《猩红猎犬》

Replace this file with the complete world setting before writing chapters.

## Setting
Pale City (苍白城) — Gothic Victorian metropolis. Gas lamps, towering
cathedrals, cobblestone streets, and an ancient catacomb network beneath.

## The Hunters' Order
The Order of the Crimson Hound — church-affiliated hunters who track
and "purify" those marked by the Blood Seal.

## The Blood Seal (血印)
Scarlet sigils that appear on the skin of the "afflicted." Officially:
sign of corruption by the Old Blood. Truth: injected via the Church's
secret Sacrament of Blood.

## The Old Blood
Ancient entity sleeping beneath the city. The Church uses its blood in
rituals, believing they can control it. The blood's consciousness is
awakening.

## Tone
Dark gothic horror. Gaslight and shadow. Body horror meets cosmic dread.
