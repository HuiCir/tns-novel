# Chapter Summaries — 《猩红猎犬》

## Chapter 01: 猩红初印
Arthur Blackwood, Hound Inquisitor, tracks a "Blood Seal witch" in Pale
City's old quarter. After the execution, he discovers injection marks on
the victim's arm — not natural corruption. That night he finds Livia Grey,
a 16-year-old girl freshly injected with the Old Blood at the church
orphanage. Her father, a church scribe, was executed for discovering the
truth. Arthur files a false report erasing her from the hunt list.

**Handoff**: Livia needs shelter. Arthur has committed treason. The Blood
Seal on Livia's arm has ~3 days before reaching her carotid. Mother Greta
in the catacombs may help. Captain Caspar suspects something is wrong.

## Chapter 02: 墓穴深处
Arthur hides Livia in the ancient catacombs beneath Pale City, where
Mother Greta — a former church nurse — shelters escapees. Greta reveals
the Sacrament of Blood: for ten years, the Church has injected orphans
and prisoners with Old Blood to create "witches" for public executions,
maintaining fear and dependence. Livia's father discovered the list of
victims. Arthur infiltrates the cathedral archives to find it. Meanwhile,
Caspar finds footprints matching Arthur's boots near the orphanage and
chooses — for now — not to report it.

**Handoff**: Arthur has entered the archives via catacomb passage. He
carries Livia's father's copper badge as a key. Caspar is wavering.
Livia has ~2 days before the Blood Seal reaches critical spread.

## Chapter 03: 猩红圣殿
Arthur finds the complete victim ledger — 237 names over ten years, each
signed by High Justiciar Severin. The final document reveals Severin's
plan: "Final Purification" — using the cathedral bells at midnight to
activate the Old Blood in ALL injected citizens simultaneously, then
purging the city in holy fire. Arthur is discovered by Severin, who
reveals he has injected himself with Old Blood for a decade and learned
to control it. Arthur escapes through a ventilation shaft. Livia has
begun hearing the Old God's whispers — speaking in ancient tongues.

**Handoff**: Severin knows Arthur's identity. The ledger and Severin's
letter are the only evidence. Livia's consciousness is being invaded.
Caspar is on the fence. Midnight is ~6 hours away.

## Chapter 04: 猎犬之牙
Caspar finds Arthur in the Sunken Cathedral and reads the evidence.
Greta arrives with 20+ refugees — former Hounds, survivors, helpers.
They form an alliance. The plan: Livia crawls through a drainage shaft
to the bell tower's gear room to sabotage the resonance mechanism.
Arthur confronts Severin at the grand altar to buy time. Caspar's team
distributes copies of the evidence across the city. The old one-eared
cartographer maps the infiltration route.

**Handoff**: Four hours until midnight. Livia must crawl alone through
the shaft. Arthur will face Severin unarmed — not to kill, but to delay.
The refugees will create diversions. Everything depends on the bell
mechanism being destroyed before the resonance activates.

## Chapter 05: 苍白黎明
Livia reaches the gear room and jams her sword into the bell mechanism,
stopping the resonance. The blade cuts through the Blood Seal on her arm,
severing the Old God's neural link — not a cure, but an interruption.
Arthur extinguishes Severin's ritual candles at the altar, causing the
Old Blood in Severin's body to backfire. Caspar arrives with knights
carrying the evidence. Severin collapses, drained by his own corrupted
blood. At dawn, Arthur and Livia stand on a hill overlooking Pale City.
The Blood Seal on Livia's arm has faded to silver scars. They walk toward
the border mountains as the sun rises — not victory, but possibility.

**Final state**: Arthur and Livia free, heading toward the Free Cities.
Caspar leading a reformed Hound order. Severin alive but broken. The Old
God still sleeping beneath the city. Evidence circulated. A crack has
opened in the Church's foundation — not destroyed, but irreversibly damaged.
